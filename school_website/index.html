Little Angels Play Home is a play group intended to provide experiences, a young child enjoys and needs for its development-physically, mentally, emotionally, and socially.



Our Aims

  • To inculate good learning and grasping habit.
  • To develop good habits of eating, cleanliness and health.
  • To help them become independent and self-confident.

It is an organized way of offering children necessary experiences that promote there all around development room to play in, materials for creative work and companionship with children of there own age. This includes play way, learning by doing songs, movement and gesture, dramatization, creative activity and celebration of festival





It is a great pleasure to welcome all of you to our play home. We have made a humble begining with play home section in the year 2005-2006 with very good encouragement from the parents. We are thankful to all the parents who have very good faith in our vision to build up a pro-active education. We hope to receive your continued co-operation in the years to come in building up this play home.



Having children is the Joy of every family.In this regard, every person strives to ensure that his or her children are brought up in the best way possible.To begin with, our play home provides facilities to make learning possible. We have enough learning equipment be it books and other reference materials or equipment used for practical lessons. We maintain good discipline as well as good moral values. It is the prayer of every parent that his or her child will be well mannered.Our Teachers are guides of learners and they highly influence what students learn. We are also very interested in the development of children. We are very eager to see their progress.
